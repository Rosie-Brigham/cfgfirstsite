Gainsborough House Social Forum



Thomas Gainsborough was an English portrait and landscape painter born in Sudbury 1727. Gainsborough House dates from the Tudor times; The original house, represented today only by the Entrance Hall and Aubrey Herbert Room, probably dates back to around 1500. The oak doorway, was probably the original front door to the house, leading straight into the Entrance Hall from the street. Now the house is an exciting tribute to Gainsborough legacy. As as holding the largesst collections of Gainsborough's works in the world, the house also details his history and the influential art he played at the high point of English landscape art. Most importantly, the house provides new and apsiring artists with the skills to follow in his footsteps, from primary school children to adult evening classes, we have it all here at GH!
